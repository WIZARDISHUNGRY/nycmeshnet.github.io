Who we are

NYC Mesh is a community owned network. Our network consists of Wi-Fi router "nodes" and "supernodes" spread throughout the city. The network has no central server and connects directly to the internet backbone. We peer directly with other major networks so we do not rely on an ISP. All nodes cooperate in the distribution of data, and our network can also stand-alone in case of emergencies or internet shutdown.

Our ASN is AS395853 and we connect to the DE-CIX internet exchange. Please contact us to peer with our network.

Our mission

We aim to create a free, resilient, stand-alone communication system that serves both for daily use and also for emergencies—be it power outages or internet disruption—running software that helps our community with hyperlocal maps and events.
